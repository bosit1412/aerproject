About PROEIT

PROEIT is an Uzbek company that provides online courses in the IT field. The company was founded in 2015 and has since become one of the leading players in the online education market in Uzbekistan. PROEIT offers courses in various IT areas, including: • Programming
• Data analytics
• Design
• Business informatics
• Internet marketing
PROEIT courses are developed by experienced lecturers and practitioners from the IT industry in Uzbekistan. They include video lectures, practical assignments, homework, and projects. Learning on PROEIT courses takes place online. This allows you to study at your own convenience and anywhere with internet access. PROEIT offers flexible payment terms for education. You can pay for your education in installments or monthly.
